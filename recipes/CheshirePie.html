Cheshire Pie

Let's Learn how to make a delicious Cheshire Pie! This is a modern interpretation of a centuries old recipe from England and and North American Colonies

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Pork
  • Apple(s)

Recipe

  1. Boil water and butter in a 2:1 ratio by ounces
  2. Mix one full egg and an additional yolk into six cups of flour
  3. slowly pour water mixture into flour
  4. knead dough, cut into thirds, and roll into base, wall, and cover
  5. let rest for 4-24 hours
  6. build coffin, prebake for 15 minutes
  7. fill with meat of choice, apples, and vegetable broth
  8. bake for an hour, and enjoy!
