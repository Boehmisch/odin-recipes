Sandwich

Now to make a delicious Bologna and Cheese Sandwich! This is a basic recipe enjoyed by many poor americans over the years

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • American Sliced Cheese
  • Slice of bologna

Recipe

  1. Get two slices of bread, a slice of bologna and a slice of american sliced cheese
  2. put the slices of bologna and cheese on one of the slices of bread
  3. put the last slice of bread on top
  4. Enjoy!
