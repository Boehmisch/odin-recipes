Now some delicous easy to make chicken snacks for your dog!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast!

Recipe

  1. preheat oven to around 375 degrees
  2. Cut chicken into strips, not too thick not too thin
  3. arrange chicken strips equidistant on tray
  4. Bake for 20 minute, or until sufficiently dry
  5. let cool, and watch your dog enjoy!
